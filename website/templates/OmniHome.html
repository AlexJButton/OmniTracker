What is OmniTracker?

This is a personal project that I have made to practice and learn more about web development (learn more).
OmniTracker is fairly self explanatory from the name, it lets you track everything!
This is a website that allows the users to create completely custom tables with whatever information they want. OmniTracker is a website idea I thought of while working on some of my college projects that I simply didn't have the time or knowledge to make at the time and is something I am making now while I am searching for a job.




How do you use OmniTracker?


Simple!
First just create an account on the signup page and then login:

(Passwords are stored with hashing for security but I would recommend against using a password you use elsewhere)

- You can then just use that account to login and everything you create/edit/delete will be saved between sessions.


Now you can make a tracker:

- Navigate to the "Make Tracker" page with the navbar at the top.
- From here you can set the name of the tracker and add/remove fields(columns) and set their type.

(Right now the only supported types are text and numbers but I want to add support for dates, times, and more. These dates and times would also include setting up automated alerts to the user when the date & time is near.)


After you make the tracker you will be directed to add the first information:

- From here you can edit the table, adding and removing entire rows or just changing the entry in each cell.
- After you are done you just need to click save at the bottom and all your changes will be kept.
- As you can see on the top right of the image you are also able to delete the tracker from this screen.


Lastly, viewing all of the trackers you have from the "View Trackers" page at the top navbar:

- In here you can click on any of the listed trackers that you have made and you will be taken to the page where you can edit them.
